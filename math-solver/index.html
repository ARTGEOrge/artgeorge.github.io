🧮 Math Solver

Type a math problem and press Enter.

Solve for x: type an equation with = and an x. Handles proportions / linear (x/23 = 20/60), quadratics (x^2 - 5x + 6 = 0), and cubics (x^3 - 6x^2 + 11x - 6 = 0) — with step-by-step working and exact fractions where possible.
Or just calculate: + - * / ^, % (remainder), parentheses, and functions sqrt, sin, cos, tan, log (base 10), ln, abs, round. Constants: pi, e.
Geometry: name a shape and give measurements as key=value — area, perimeter, volume & surface area with a labelled diagram.
2-D: rectangle l=8 w=5, square s=4, triangle b=6 h=4 (or a=3 b=4 c=5), circle r=7, parallelogram b=6 h=4, trapezoid a=4 b=8 h=5, rhombus d1=6 d2=8.
3-D: cube s=4, cuboid l=4 w=3 h=2, sphere r=5, cylinder r=3 h=10, cone r=3 h=4, pyramid b=6 h=9. Type just the shape name to see its formulas.
