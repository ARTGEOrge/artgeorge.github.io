🔐 Cipher lock & unlock text and numbers with a secret key
🔐 Secret message
Type a message or numbers, choose a secret key, and Encrypt. To read an encrypted message, paste it, enter the same key, and Decrypt.
🔒 Uses real AES‑256‑GCM encryption with a key derived from your password (PBKDF2, 150k rounds). Everything happens on your device — nothing is sent anywhere. Keep your key safe: without the exact same key, an encrypted message can't be recovered.
