⬆️ ResUp

Upscale any video to 1080p, 2K, or 4K — from a YouTube link or your own file — with a Fast (Lanczos) engine or true AI super-resolution (Real-ESRGAN).

Checking if ResUp is running…

ResUp runs locally on your computer (it downloads and encodes video on your machine), so it isn't a hosted website. Launch it by running start.bat in C:\code\video-upscaler, then click Open ResUp above. Files save to your Downloads folder.
